I am a postgraduate researcher at University of Moratuwa (UoM). I am co-advised by Prof.Samitha Samaranayake, Dr.Amal Shehan Perera and Dr.Uthayasankar Thayasivam. I am a member of the Data Science, Engineering & Analytics Research group of UoM. I completed my B.Sc in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka in 2017.

My research interests lie in the intersection of mobility, artificial intelligence, algorithmic and system optimizations. In parallel to that, I have worked on applying natural language processing and understanding in the legal domain. In my current research, I am interested in enabling efficient and sustainable urban transportation systems, by utilizing advances in information technology, mathematical modeling and optimization, and new transportation paradigms such as mobility-on-demand systems.

My researches are generously supported by Digital Mobility Solutions Lanka (Pvt) Ltd (2017/2018 and 2018/2019).
